“Don't make gender balance an afterthought”

(JL Martin, “Ten Simple Rules to Achieve Conference Speaker Gender Balance”. PLoS Comput Biol 10(11): e1003903)

The XPRAG.it 2018 Organizing Committee strives to achieve gender balance in all aspects of this conference. The Organizing Committee endeavors to achieve this balance through equal female and male representation on the Program Committee and as session chairs, as well as through invitations to high quality speakers of both sexes in line with the gender balance in our research community.
